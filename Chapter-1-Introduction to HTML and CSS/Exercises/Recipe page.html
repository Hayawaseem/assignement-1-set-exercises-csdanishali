Homemade Pizza

With this recipe, enjoy creating delicious, cheesy, and fresh pizza at home!

ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 packet or 2 tsp of active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup of wark water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp of sugar
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings (chicken, bell peppers, mushrooms, olives etc...)

Pizza Sauce Recipe

Make your own fresh and tasty pizza sauce at home

  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
